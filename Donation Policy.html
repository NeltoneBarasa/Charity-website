Donation Policy

This policy explains how we receive, steward, and report on funds.

Use of Funds

  • Programs and services: 80–90% (education, healthcare, water, environment)
  • Administration and compliance: 5–10%
  • Fundraising and outreach: 5–10%

Receipts and Records

All donations are receipted. Aggregated impact and financial summaries are published annually on this site.

Restricted Gifts

We honor donor intent where feasible. If a project is fully funded or unfeasible, remaining funds are reallocated to the nearest mission-aligned need.

Refunds

Refunds may be considered within 14 days in cases of error. Contact info@osekofoundation.org.
