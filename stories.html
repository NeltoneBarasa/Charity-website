Water Project
Tanzania December 2024

From 5km Walk to Clean Water at Home

Meet Amina, a mother of three who used to walk 5km daily to fetch water. Thanks to our borehole project, her village now has clean water within 200 meters of every home.

"My children no longer miss school because of water collection. They have time to study and play. This borehole changed our entire village."
Impact:
500+ families now have access to clean water, reducing waterborne diseases by 80%
Education
Western Kenya November 2024

First Generation Student Becomes Teacher

Samuel was the first in his village to complete secondary school, thanks to our scholarship program. Today, he's back teaching at the same school that changed his life.

"I never thought I'd become a teacher. Now I'm helping other children achieve their dreams, just like I did."
Impact:
50+ students have graduated through our scholarship program, with 15 now employed locally
Healthcare
Uganda October 2024

Mother Gives Birth Safely in Remote Village

Grace gave birth to twins in our mobile health clinic, the first safe delivery in her village's history. Previously, women had to travel 50km for maternal care.

"I was so scared when I learned I was having twins. The clinic saved my life and my babies' lives. We are so grateful."
Impact:
200+ safe deliveries, 1,000+ children vaccinated, maternal mortality reduced by 60%
