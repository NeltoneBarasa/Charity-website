Donation Questions

How is my donation used?

Your donation is immediately put to work in communities across East Africa. We use a transparent 4-step process: 1) Your donation ‚Üí 2) Local procurement ‚Üí 3) Distribution ‚Üí 4) Impact reporting. 95% of all donations go directly to programs, with only 5% for administrative costs.

Example Impact: $50 provides clean water for 2 families for one month, or school meals for 25 children.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes! Oseko Foundation is a registered NGO (NGO/UG/0012345) and donations are tax-deductible in many countries. After making a donation, you'll receive an automatic tax receipt via email. For annual tax purposes, we can provide consolidated donation reports upon request.

Please consult with your tax advisor for specific deductibility in your country.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple secure payment methods for your convenience:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express
  • PayPal: Safe and secure online payments
  • Bitcoin: Cryptocurrency donations
  • Bank Transfer: Direct bank deposits for large donations

Can I set up recurring monthly donations?

Absolutely! Monthly giving is our most impactful way to donate. Monthly donations provide:

  • Consistent Support: Reliable funding for ongoing programs
  • Cost Effective: Lower administrative costs, more impact
  • Easy Management: Modify or cancel anytime
