Lifesaving Services for Children

We've pioneered programs that treat children close to home, saving lives before it's too late. Even before the COVID-19 pandemic, millions of children suffered from preventable diseases like pneumonia, malaria, and diarrhea. Now, high levels of hunger and malnutrition are even more widespread. At Oseko Foundation, we know the world's children deserve better.
