Our Mission & Vision

Founded in 2017, Oseko Foundation is a registered Community Based Organization driven by a passion to create positive, sustainable change in East Africa. We believe in empowering communities to break the cycle of poverty and build a future filled with opportunity through locally-led development initiatives.

Clean Water Access

Installing sustainable water systems and purification facilities to bring safe, accessible water to underserved communities across East Africa

Healthcare Services

Operating mobile health clinics and building local healthcare capacity to deliver essential medical services to remote communities

Quality Education

Building schools, training teachers, and providing educational resources to ensure every child has access to quality learning opportunities

Environmental Sustainability

Implementing reforestation projects and clean energy solutions to ensure long-term community resilience and environmental protection

Our Vision & Goals

Our commitment to transforming East African communities

Our Vision

To create thriving, self-sufficient communities across East Africa where every individual has access to quality education, healthcare, clean water, and economic opportunities.

Our Goal

To transform 100,000+ lives across East Africa by 2030 through sustainable community development programs, focusing on education access, healthcare improvement, clean water initiatives, and economic empowerment opportunities.

Our Journey & Key Achievements

Milestones that mark our commitment to East African communities

2017

Foundation Established

Started as grassroots initiative with focus on education support in Western Kenya. Registered as Community Based Organization in Uganda.

2019

Program Expansion

Launched healthcare mobile clinics and water access projects. Expanded operations to Tanzania with community development programs.

2021

Major Impact Milestone

Reached 25,000+ beneficiaries across three countries. Established partnerships with 15+ local organizations and international donors.

2023

Sustainability Focus

Launched environmental conservation programs and solar energy projects. Received NGO verification and transparency awards.

2024

Record Impact Year

Served 50,000+ people, built 20+ schools, established 15+ health clinics. Annual fundraising gala raised $150,000+.

2025

Excellence & Expansion Year

Launched digital transformation initiative reaching 10,000+ additional beneficiaries through mobile technology. Expanded clean water projects to serve 25+ new communities across East Africa. Strengthened financial transparency with real-time impact reporting.
