Our Mission & Vision

As an East African based organization, Oseko Foundation is driven by a passion to create positive, sustainable change in our region. We believe in empowering communities to break the cycle of poverty and build a future filled with opportunity.

Clean Water Access

Bringing safe and accessible water sources to underserved communities across East Africa

Environmental Sustainability

Implementing projects that ensure long-term community resilience and environmental protection

Our core focus area

Making a difference where it matters most across Kenya, Uganda, and Tanzania

Education

Transforming lives through quality education, vocational training, and hands-on learning experiences. We build schools, train teachers, and provide practical skills training including sewing, crafts, and technical skills that empower communities across East Africa.

Learn More

Healthcare

Providing lifesaving medical services, mobile health clinics, and essential healthcare infrastructure. We focus on maternal care, child health, disease prevention, and bringing medical services to the most remote communities across East Africa.

Learn More

Environment

Protecting our planet through comprehensive environmental programs including reforestation, clean energy solutions, and climate education. We plant trees, install solar power systems, and educate communities about sustainable practices for a greener future.
