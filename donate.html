About Our Impact

Similar to a mutual fund investment, funds are managed by Charity Navigator analysts to include only the most efficient and effective charities. These funds employ a comprehensive and balanced strategy to address the core issues of the specific cause.

Our group applies a range of effective approaches proven to be impactful at addressing the issue at hand. This includes an intentional mix of charities that implement evidence-based strategies, ranging from direct services to regional coalitions to national policy reform, with the resources allocated to maximize impact.

Our analysts manage and assess these funds on a quarterly basis to ensure that the most highly rated and efficient charities receive your support.
