Sustainable solutions for climate-vulnerable communities

Oseko Foundation is committed to improving living standards in underserved and climate-vulnerable regions of Tanzania, particularly in semi-arid and rural communities where access to clean water, healthcare, sanitation, and education remains limited. Our approach focuses on sustainable solutions that strengthen local capacity and promote long-term self-sufficiency.

üíß Water Access Projects

In many parts of central and northern Tanzania, prolonged droughts and unreliable rainfall force families to travel long distances for unsafe water.

Focus Areas

  • Dodoma Region (Chamwino & Kondoa districts)
  • Singida Region
  • Manyara Region (Simanjiro & Kiteto districts)

Our Interventions

  • Construction and rehabilitation of boreholes
  • Installation of hand-pump and solar-powered water systems
  • Community training on water management and maintenance

These projects reduce waterborne diseases, ease the burden on women and children, and restore dignity through reliable access to clean water.

üè• Healthcare Services

Access to healthcare remains a major challenge in remote Tanzanian communities, where clinics are scarce and distances are long.

Target Regions:

Tabora Shinyanga Rural Dodoma Rural Singida

Key Actions:

  • Supporting the establishment of basic rural health clinics
  • Providing essential medical supplies
  • Partnering with local health workers to strengthen service delivery

üöø Sanitation and Hygiene Training

Poor sanitation and limited hygiene awareness contribute significantly to preventable illnesses, particularly among children.

Our Sanitation Programs Focus On:

  • Hygiene education for families and schoolchildren
  • Training on safe waste disposal and handwashing practices
  • Community-led initiatives to improve sanitation facilities

These efforts are especially active in water-scarce communities where sanitation challenges are most severe.
